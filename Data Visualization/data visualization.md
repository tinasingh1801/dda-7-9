# *Data Visualization*
## What is Data Visualization?
1. **Data visualization** is the graphical representation of information and data.  
2. Additionally, it provides an excellent way for employees or business owners to present data to non-technical audiences without confusion.
3. In the world of Big Data, data visualization tools and technologies are essential to analyze massive amounts of information and make data-driven decisions.
4. The importance of data visualization is simple: it helps people see, interact with, and better understand data. 
5. Whether simple or complex, the right visualization can bring everyone on the same page, regardless of their level of expertise.
6. Effective data visualization is a delicate balancing act between form and function.
